üîÑ Common Admin Workflows

Adding a New Faculty Member

  1. Navigate to Faculty Management
  2. Click "Add New Faculty"
  3. Fill in details (name, designation, department, etc.)
  4. Upload faculty photo
  5. Set visibility and save
  6. Faculty appears on public site immediately

Publishing Exam Results

  1. Go to Results Upload
  2. Select academic year and class
  3. Choose exam type (Mid-term, Final, etc.)
  4. Upload student results (CSV or enter manually)
  5. Review and verify data
  6. Publish to make visible to students/parents

Creating an Announcement

  1. Access Notices Management
  2. Click "Create New Notice"
  3. Enter title and content (rich text editor)
  4. Set priority (High/Medium/Low)
  5. Choose category and dates
  6. Publish - appears on homepage and notices page
